Cultural Heritage

Ambalangoda Mask Museum

Discover Sri Lanka’s centuries-old mask-making tradition at the Ambalangoda Mask Museum. Watch artisans craft vibrant masks used in traditional dance and rituals — a true reflection of local artistry and folklore.

01

Nature Experience

Madu River Safari

Cruise through the serene waters of the Madu River surrounded by lush mangroves, tiny islands, and diverse birdlife. Perfect for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

02

Local Craftsmanship

Meetiyagoda Moonstone Mines

Visit Sri Lanka’s only moonstone mine in nearby Meetiyagoda. Explore the gemstone excavation process and witness how the world-famous blue moonstones are polished into dazzling jewelry.

03

Wildlife Conservation

Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Just a short drive away, the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery offers a unique opportunity to see endangered sea turtles and learn about Sri Lanka’s conservation efforts to protect these gentle creatures.
